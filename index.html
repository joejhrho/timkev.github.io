Food Safety system

Timkev International top management recognizes the importance of food safety throughout the food supply chain particularly at all stages where Timkev International performs raw material sourcing, storage, handling, processing and selling. Everyone within the organization has the collective responsibility of food safety and has a moral obligation to produce safe, legal and authentic products to our customers and the consumers. A positive food safety culture has been nurturing within the organization. Timkev International is committed to taking all responsible steps and precautions and exercising our due diligence to protect and preserve the human food chain in our custody. To ensure best practice Timkev International has established Food Safety & Defense Plans for our products based on the HACCP and participate in internationally recognized BRC Global Standard for Food Safety audits.
